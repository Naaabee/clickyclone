About us

We are a small team of keyboard enthusiasts from Singapore. After building multiple custom keyboards for friends and friends of friends, we decided to launch a bespoke mechanical keyboard building service for the masses. We hope to serve as a launch pad for people to get into custom keyboards, and join the great community surrounding it.

Many of us spend hours a day on the computer. Invest in a quality product that is functional yet elegant and start having a great typing experience everyday.

Consult us to turn your vision into reality, we will guide you along the way till you receive the final custom keyboard in the flesh.
